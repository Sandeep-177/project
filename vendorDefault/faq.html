Frequently Asked Questions

We aim to provide vendors with a seamless experience while offering their services. Below, you'll find answers to commonly asked questions categorized into different sections for clarity. Whether you're new to our platform or an existing vendor, these FAQs will help you understand our processes better.

General

What is Smart Home Service?

Smart Home Service is an online platform that connects skilled professionals with customers looking for home services such as AC repair, home painting, plumbing, and cleaning. Vendors can list their services, receive customer requests, and get paid securely.

How can I register as a vendor?

To register, visit our Vendor Registration Page, fill in the required details, select your services, and choose a subscription plan. Once your profile is verified, you can start receiving customer requests.

Is there a registration fee for vendors?

No, registering on our platform is free. However, to start receiving service requests, vendors must subscribe to one of our affordable plans, ensuring long-term business growth.

Can I provide multiple services?

Yes, you can offer multiple services based on your expertise. During registration, you can select all relevant services, and you can update your offerings anytime from your vendor dashboard.

How do I get customer requests?

Once your account is active, customers can find you based on your location and services. When a customer books a service, you'll receive a notification, and you can accept or decline the request.

How do I communicate with customers?

After accepting a service request, you can communicate with the customer through our in-app messaging or direct contact (if enabled), ensuring smooth coordination.

Can I pause my services temporarily?

Yes, if you need to take a break, you can temporarily deactivate your account or mark yourself unavailable in your dashboard. You can resume services whenever you're ready.

Is my personal information secure?

Absolutely. We prioritize data security and ensure that your personal and financial information is protected through industry-standard encryption and privacy policies.

Fees & Charges

Does Smart Home Service charge any commission?

Yes, we charge a small commission on every successful booking to cover operational costs. The commission percentage is transparent and will be deducted before the final payout.

Are there any hidden charges?

No, all charges are clearly mentioned during registration and in your dashboard. We believe in complete transparency with vendors to build long-term partnerships.

How do I check my earnings?

Your dashboard provides a real-time view of your earnings, completed services, and pending payments. You can track transactions and request withdrawals anytime.

What happens if a customer cancels a service?

If a customer cancels before the service begins, you will not be charged any commission. If they cancel after service initiation, a partial or full compensation policy may apply based on the case.

How can I withdraw my earnings?

You can request a payout anytime from your dashboard. Payments are processed securely via bank transfer, UPI, or digital wallet, depending on your preference.

Managing Your Account

How do I update my profile details?

You can log in to your vendor dashboard, go to the "Profile" section, and update details such as your name, phone number, service areas, and skills.

Can I change my service location?

Yes, if you move to a new city or expand your service area, you can update your location in the dashboard to start receiving requests in your new area.

What should I do if I forget my password?

Click on the "Forgot Password" option on the login page, enter your registered email or phone number, and follow the instructions to reset your password.

How do I deactivate my account?

If you want to temporarily or permanently stop receiving service requests, you can go to your Account Settings and select Deactivate Account.

Can I switch between different plans?

Yes, you can upgrade your plan anytime to get more benefits, such as better rankings and more customer leads.

How do I report a problem with my account?

If you face any issues, you can contact our support team via email, chat, or phone, and we will assist you promptly.

Service

How do I manage my service requests?

Your dashboard allows you to view new, pending, and completed service requests. You can accept, schedule, or decline requests based on your availability.

What if I can't complete a booked service?

If you are unable to fulfill a service request, inform the customer and support team immediately. Repeated cancellations may affect your account performance.

How do I receive service ratings and reviews?

After completing a service, customers can rate and review your work. High ratings improve your visibility and help attract more customers.

Can I list a service that is not on the platform?

Currently, we only support predefined services, but you can suggest new service categories by contacting our support team.

Do I need to bring my own tools?

Yes, vendors are expected to bring their own tools and materials unless the service specifically mentions otherwise.

Can I reject a service request?

Yes, if you're unavailable or not interested in a job, you can decline a request without any penalty.

Fulfillment by Smart Home Service

What is Fulfillment by Smart Home Service?

This is our premium service where we handle all customer interactions, bookings, and payments while assigning service requests to top-rated vendors.

How do I qualify for Fulfillment by Smart Home Service?

To qualify, you need to maintain high ratings, low cancellations, and a good service completion rate. Selected vendors get priority bookings and additional benefits.

How does the payout system work for fulfillment services?

For fulfillment services, we collect payments from customers and transfer earnings to vendors after deducting service fees.

Can I opt out of Fulfillment services?

Yes, you can choose not to participate in this feature. However, vendors who opt-in get more business opportunities.

What happens if a customer is unhappy with my service?

We encourage vendors to resolve disputes professionally. If needed, our support team will mediate to find a fair resolution.

Do I need special certification for fulfillment services?

Certain premium services may require certification or verification, which will be informed during the onboarding process.
